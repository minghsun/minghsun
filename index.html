I’m Minghsun Li, currently a senior designer at Yahoo leading EC horizontal design team. Previously, I worked for a start-up department in Trend Micro and cofounded Olis before that. I also designed Inspr and built Push News as side projects. I love to explore the possibilities of design and am interested in what products can bring to people. I leverage my backgrounds of CS, animation and art to design and build products. I help products and business grow with goal-oriented design and impact users with emotional design approaches. I am always excited to seek challenges from anywhere to make products that people want.

As for interests, I love animations, especially short ones. I made an animated short film, “The Little Pinky” when I was a student, which I’m very proud of. Besides, I also love making things in wood.
