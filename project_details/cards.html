Loyalty Cards App Concept, 2014

We were making a loyalty card app which users can collect all the loyalty card in without having too many cards in their wallets. This was a UI experiment. I tried to provide meaningful interactions for the users to understand the screen transition. In the end, this wasn't implemented. But I had a lot of fun exploring new ways of interaction. This is the prototype made with FramerJS you can play with.
